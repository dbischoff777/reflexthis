## Steam **INT stats** (create these)

Copy/paste these **API Names** into Steamworks → *Stats* (Type `INT`, Set By `Client`, Default `0`; “Increment Only” recommended for all of them):

```text
STAT_BEST_SCORE
STAT_BEST_COMBO
STAT_FASTEST_REACTION_MS
STAT_GAMES_PLAYED
STAT_PLAYTIME_SECONDS
STAT_PLAYER_LEVEL
STAT_MODE_REFLEX_PLAYED
STAT_MODE_SEQUENCE_PLAYED
STAT_MODE_SURVIVAL_PLAYED
STAT_MODE_NIGHTMARE_PLAYED
```
